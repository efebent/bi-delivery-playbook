# BI Delivery Playbook

This playbook provides a practical and lightweight methodology for managing Business Intelligence (BI) projects.

It focuses on:
- Clear delivery lifecycle
- Change-driven methodology
- Right-sized governance (S / M / L)
- Business and technical alignment

## Who is this for?

- BI Managers and Team Leads  
- BI Consultants  
- Analytics Engineers  
- Organizations delivering BI projects to internal or external customers

## How to use this playbook

This is not a rigid framework.

Use it as:
- A reference model
- A starting point
- A foundation to adapt for your own BI organization
